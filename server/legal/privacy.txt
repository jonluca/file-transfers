File Share Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 8, 2026

File Share only uses the information needed to sign you in, transfer files, host files you choose to share, and manage subscriptions.
This can include your account details, basic profile information, file transfer metadata, hosted files, and purchase status.
Hosted files are stored only to provide the download link you request and may be deleted when they expire or are removed.
We do not sell your personal information.
We may use service providers for authentication, cloud storage, and subscription management.
If you want your account or hosted data removed, use the app controls or the support contact listed with the app.
